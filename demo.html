Training
Video of the our Game.
- Functions of the buttons
- Approve: When you are sure about the choice you made.
- Deny: When you are not sure about the choice you made and choose again.
- Main Menu: When you want to go back to the Main Menu.
- Pause: Contains "Resume," "Restart," and "Exit Game."
- View ID: When you want to check about the information of that customer.
- Choices: Where you will choose the choice to interact with the customer.
- How does the game work?
- When interacting with the customer, you need to check ID to know the information about them when they are going to check in. And when you are done checking the information, you need to know what they want or need, and after that, you need to choose what to answer them at the right corner of the screen.
- Choosing a correct answer(accept), the accuracy (100%) at the top right will remain the same, but if you choose the wrong one, it will decrease. Also, there is a score. of how many points you got; when the shift is done, you can see your remarks if you've passed the stage or not.
